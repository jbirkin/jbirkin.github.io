Analysis and modeling of multidimensional datasets

In the modern era of data-driven research, resolving the internal structures of distant galaxies is becoming increasingly feasible and essential for understanding their properties. My work involves analyzing complex, multidimensional datasets that combine spatial and spectral information, effectively creating 3D maps of these galaxies. To facilitate this analysis, I developed a custom tool for modeling emission features on a pixel-by-pixel basis, enabling more detailed insights into the data. The tool, designed for high-resolution datasets, is publicly availablehere.
